Humans of VSA
Humans of VSA (HoVSA) is an ongoing project UVSASE is revamping for the 2020 year and, hopefully, for many more years to come. Inspired by Humans of New York, the aim of this project is to highlight various VSA members from each of our 19 affiliated schools and have them give a glimpse on the life they currently live. Through this project, HoVSA hopes to:
  1. Improve engagement from members to their respective school
  2. Improve engagement from schools to UVSASE
  3. Cultivate a closer community within the Southeast region
  4. Increase one’s awareness to the daily lives of the people around us

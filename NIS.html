Through skits, dances, songs, and more, Night in Saigon is a student-organized show dedicated to promoting Vietnamese culture and heritage to the audience. We aim not only to educate those who are interested in learning more about the culture, but to also raise awareness about the ever-changing Vietnamese-American identity.

Every year, Night in Saigon features a different theme which shapes the direction of our show and the message we wish to convey to the audience. Last year’s theme was “Seize the Moment,” or “Nắm Lấy Khoảnh Khắc.”

Vietnamese Student Association

In 2002, the University of Georgia's Vietnamese Student Association (UGA VSA) was established in order to preserve the Vietnamese culture and heritage through cultural awareness and to educate those who are interested in learning our customs, practices, and interests.

Our organization creates and stimulates awareness of the transforming Vietnamese-American cultural identity, while also fostering friendship, goodwill, and a sense of community among our members of all racial and ethnic backgrounds at UGA.

Our Pillars

Culture

Promote Vietnamese culture and exhort the edification of the traditions among students

Community

Recognize and promote the necessity of community services

Friendship

Improve relations between students through means of social gatherings, meetings, and service activities

Leadership

Encourage and advance the leadership development of young professionals

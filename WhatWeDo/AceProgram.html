ACE Program

Anh Chi Em (ACE) is a Big/Little Program that the UGA VSA offers. The ACE program aims to promote friendship and unity within the larger community within VSA by providing activities and events that allow “Bigs” to bond with their “Littles.”

Every year, interested mentees can sign up to be apart of an ACE family. These mentees are then sorted into a “family” and are led by a family leader. Throughout the ACE program, friendships and memories are made within and beyond VSA.

Partners and Awards

UGA VSA would not be where is it today without the vital support of our partners. We are very thankful for them as they have allowed us to create a productive environment at UGA for all of our amazing members.

Each year our partners recognize organizations that go above and beyond their expectations. We are very proud to say that UGA VSA has done an incredible job and earned numerous awards from our partners!

UVSASE

The Union of Vietnamese Student Associations of the Southeast (UVSASE) is a non-profit, non-partisan, community-based organization with a commitment to unify Vietnamese youth organizations in the southeast region.

  • Jackie Minus International Student Organization of the Year 2018/19
  • Outstanding Advertisement & Promotion 2018/19
  • Outstanding Organization Leader 2018/19: Summer Phung
  • Outstanding Community Service & Philanthropy 2017/18
  • Outstanding Community Service & Philanthropy 2016/17
  • Outstanding Organization Leader 2016/17: Brian Xu
  • Honorable Mention: ISL Organization of the Year 2016/17
ISL

The department of International Student Life (ISL) enhances the student‐learning environment through programs and services that internationalize the campus experience. This work is accomplished through focused and intentional efforts.

  • Most Outstanding VSA in the Southeast 2017/18
  • Most Outstanding Performance in the Southeast 2017/18
  • Most Outstanding Philanthropy in the Southeast 2017/18
UNAVSA

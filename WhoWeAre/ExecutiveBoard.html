Executive Board

Meet the passionate individuals who have allowed the Vietnamese Student Association to be what is it today. With the dedication and commitment of all past and present executive boards, UGA VSA has been able to grow from a small club to one of the largest organizations in International Student Life.

The rapid growth can be attributed to the numerous opportunities that VSA provides to become actively involved such as the Anh Chi Em (ACE) program, general body meetings, fundraisers, volunteering, Night in Saigon (NIS), leadership conferences, and much more!

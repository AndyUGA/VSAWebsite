Olympics

VSA Olympics is a one day event that is hosted by the Union of the Vietnamese Student Asscoation of the South East (UVSASE ). All of the VSAs in the South East region participate and it serves as an opportunity for members to strengthen connections within the region through friendly competition in volleyball, flag football, soccer, tug-of-war, and an obstacle course.

By providing a lighthearted atmosphere filled with engaging activities, the goal is to promote camaraderie among our members while also highlighting physical fitness and ultimately having fun.
